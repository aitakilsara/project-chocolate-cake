Chocolate Cake

★★★★★

A rich, moist and delicious chocolate cake for all occasions.

👩‍🍳 Directions

🧁 For the Chocolate Cake:

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 180°C. Lightly grease your cake pan with a small amount of butter and dust it with a thin layer of flour to ensure easy release.
  2. Whisk eggs & sugar: In a large mixing bowl, vigorously whisk the whole eggs and sugar together until the mixture turns pale, slightly fluffy, and doubles in volume, slowly pour in the milk and vegetable oil, whisking gently to combine all the liquid ingredients.
  3. Fold in dry ingredients: Sift the flour, cacao powder, and baking powder directly over the wet mixture. Fold gently to keep the batter light and completely lump-free.
  4. Bake to perfection: Pour the batter into your prepared pan and bake for approximately 20 minutes. The center of the cake should remain slightly moist to keep it incredibly rich and fudgy!

🍫 For the Chocolate Ganache:

  1. Warm the cream: In a small saucepan, bring the heavy cream (100ml) to a gentle simmer over medium-low heat. Do not let it boil.
  2. Make the ganache: Place the finely chopped dark chocolate (200g) in a heatproof bowl. Pour the warm heavy cream over the chocolate and let it sit undisturbed for 1 minute to melt.
  3. Add butter & finish: Whisk the cream and chocolate gently from the center outward until a smooth, shiny glaze forms. Add the butter (20g) and stir until completely melted and glossy.
  4. Assemble: Pour the warm, silky ganache over your cooled chocolate cake, spreading it evenly with a spatula. Let it set before slicing and serving!
